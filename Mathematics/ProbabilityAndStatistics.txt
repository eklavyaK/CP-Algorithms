Conditional Probability:
    P(A/B) = P(A^B)/P(B)

Independent events:
    P(A/B) = P(A)
    P(A^B) = P(A)P(B)

Bay's Theorem:
                                     P(A)*P(B/A)
    P(A/B)     =              ------------------------
                               Total probability of B

Division of Card Deck of 52 cards:
    26 Red  and 26 Black
    Black has 13 spades and 13 clubs
    Red has 13 Diamonds and 13 Hearts
    
    Each pack of 13 cards are called suits
    Each 13 pack has 1 king , 1 queen, 1 jack, 1 ace and 9 numbered cards from 2 to 10
    King, queen and jack are called face cards
